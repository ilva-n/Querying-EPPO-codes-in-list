Find EPPO codes for the list of scientific names

Here you can query botanical names in batches. After first search you will get what you would also (and even better) get in EPPO data services page - EPPO codes for botanical names when they were found by exact match. If there will be any not matching names in your list, in second search this application will try to modify your botanical names and find what they would match.
